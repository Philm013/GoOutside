About Earth Day Everyday

Mission

Earth Day Everyday helps people explore, identify, and record local biodiversity with location-aware tools, guided identification, and a personal wildlife journal.

Data & Source Attribution

  • Species, observations, taxonomy, and photos: iNaturalist API and iNaturalist community content. API docs · Terms
  • Map framework: Leaflet. leafletjs.com
  • Basemap tiles: OpenStreetMap data and CARTO basemaps. OSM attribution · CARTO attribution
  • Bird audio identification: BirdNET model and labels (processed on-device in this app). birdnet.cornell.edu

Knowledge Graph Identification

The “Key It Out” tools (including fish and shell/mollusk flows) use trait-based narrowing plus nearby iNaturalist records. Results are candidate suggestions and should be verified before formal reporting.

Privacy

Bird audio processing is performed on-device. Your personal observations are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share data through external platforms.
